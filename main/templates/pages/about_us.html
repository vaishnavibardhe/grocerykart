About Us

HOME / ABOUT US

SAHARKAR SAOJI MASALE is a reputable spices manufacturer known for producing genuine Saoji spices. With the focus on purity and hygiene we provide high-quality products that customers trust. Committed to society responsibility, Our brand embraces the philosophy of PAY BACK TO SOCIETY. I am excited to share our progress in our mission to provide employment to those in need. Thanks to your support and suggtions, we have achieved significant milestones so far. To date, we have directly employed over 20 individuals and have indirectly contributed to the employment of 25 more through our initiatives. Looking ahead, our goal over the next decade is ambitious yet feasible: we aim to create over 100 direct jobs and facilitate more than 500 indirect job opportunities over the next decade.

Empowering the Community
Our commitment goes beyond employment; we are actively focusing on rural women’s empowerment by integrating local artisans into our production chain. By sourcing raw ingredients directly from local farmers, we ensure sustainable livelihoods while maintaining the "Farm to Fork" purity of our masalas.

New Product Launch: 2026 Edition
In line with our vision to innovate, we are thrilled to announce the launch of our newest specialty blends:
Saharkar Saoji Fish Curry Masala: A smoky, tangy blend designed for the perfect coastal-style Saoji experience.
Saharkar Saoji Egg Curry Masala: A versatile, fiery spice mix that brings professional kitchen flavours to everyday meals.
Premium Special Sabji Masala: Now available in convenient Pack of 10 pouches for consistent freshness in every vegetarian dish.

Your ongoing support and belief in our vision are deeply appreciated. Together, we can continue to make a meaningful difference in the lives of many individuals and their communities. Thank you for being part of this journey with us.

Founder & Visionary

Shivanand Saharkar

The foundation of Saharkar Saoji Masale was laid by Mr. Shivanand Saharkar, whose vision was to bring authentic Saoji spices to people while preserving the rich culinary heritage. Additionally Our Social Vision is to Pay Back to Society, his dream continues to inspire every step we take.

Co-Founder & Taste Expert

Indu Saharkar

Behind every perfect blend is the expertise of Mrs. Indu Saharkar, whose deep knowledge of traditional recipes and passion for cooking ensures that every product carries the genuine taste and aroma of homemade Saoji masala.

Proprietor

Vrushabh Saharkar

Leading the brand forward is Mr. Vrushabh Saharkar, who has transformed this family dream into a growing business. With a strong focus on quality, hygiene, and customer trust, he is committed to expanding the reach of Saharkar Saoji Masale while staying true to its roots. We strongly believe in giving back to society. As part of our commitment, Saharkar Saoji Masale aims to create meaningful impact by generating 100+ direct and indirect employment opportunities, supporting livelihoods and contributing to the growth of our community. At Saharkar Saoji Masale, we don’t just make spices — we deliver tradition, trust, and taste in every packet, while building a better future for society.
