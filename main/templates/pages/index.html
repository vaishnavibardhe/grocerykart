Founder & Visionary

Shivanand Saharkar

The foundation of Saharkar Saoji Masale was laid by Mr. Shivanand Saharkar, whose vision was to bring authentic Saoji spices to people while preserving the rich culinary heritage. Additionally Our Social Vision is to Pay Back to Society, his dream continues to inspire every step we take.

Co-Founder & Taste Expert

Indu Saharkar

Behind every perfect blend is the expertise of Mrs. Indu Saharkar, whose deep knowledge of traditional recipes and passion for cooking ensures that every product carries the genuine taste and aroma of homemade Saoji masala.

Proprietor

Vrushabh Saharkar

Leading the brand forward is Mr. Vrushabh Saharkar, who has transformed this family dream into a growing business. With a strong focus on quality, hygiene, and customer trust, he is committed to expanding the reach of Saharkar Saoji Masale while staying true to its roots. We strongly believe in giving back to society. As part of our commitment, Saharkar Saoji Masale aims to create meaningful impact by generating 100+ direct and indirect employment opportunities, supporting livelihoods and contributing to the growth of our community. At Saharkar Saoji Masale, we don’t just make spices — we deliver tradition, trust, and taste in every packet, while building a better future for society.
